## Learned
How branches, commits, and pull requests fit together in a collaborative workflow — and why pulling `main` regularly matters.

## Takeaway
My biggest "aha" was understanding why you pull regularly to update your local `main`. When teammates are working on the same project in different branches, pulling keeps your copy in sync with theirs — so when it's time to merge, your work hasn't drifted so far from everyone else's that reconciling the changes becomes painful.
